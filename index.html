Our Story

The Spirit of Military Cuisine

Reddy Gari Military Hotel serves authentic Telangana traditional food with rich spices and homely flavors. We specialize in fresh, hygienic and affordable meals including mutton, chicken, and country-style curries prepared daily — straight from the heart of Telangana to your plate.
